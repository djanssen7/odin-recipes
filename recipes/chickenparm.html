Chicken Parmesan

A classic Italian-American dish with crispy chicken, marinara sauce, and melted cheese.

Ingredients

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 large eggs (beaten)
  • 1 cup breadcrumbs (Italian seasoned)
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 cup marinara sauce
  • 1 1/2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Olive oil for frying
  • Fresh basil (optional, for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Slice chicken breasts in half lengthwise to create 4 thinner cutlets.
  3. Season chicken with salt and pepper.
  4. Dredge chicken in flour, dip in egg, then coat in breadcrumbs mixed with Parmesan cheese.
  5. In a skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Fry chicken until golden brown on both sides.
  6. Place fried chicken on a baking sheet. Spoon marinara sauce on each piece, then top with mozzarella cheese.
  7. Bake for 15–20 minutes, or until cheese is melted and bubbly.
  8. Garnish with fresh basil and serve over pasta or with a salad.
