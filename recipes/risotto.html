How to make a risotto

A rich and creamy Italian rice dish made with Arborio rice, broth, and Parmesan cheese.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups Arborio rice
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup dry white wine (optional)
  • 4 cups chicken or vegetable broth (kept warm)
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley or chives (optional, for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Heat olive oil and 1 tablespoon butter in a large pan over medium heat.
  2. Sauté the onion until soft and translucent, about 5 minutes.
  3. Add garlic and cook for 1 more minute.
  4. Stir in the Arborio rice and toast it for 1–2 minutes, stirring constantly.
  5. Add the white wine and stir until it is mostly absorbed (skip this step if not using wine).
  6. Begin adding the warm broth, 1/2 cup at a time, stirring often.
  7. Wait until the liquid is mostly absorbed before adding more broth. Repeat until the rice is tender and creamy (about 18–20 minutes).
  8. Stir in the remaining butter and Parmesan cheese. Season with salt and pepper.
  9. Serve hot, garnished with herbs if desired.
